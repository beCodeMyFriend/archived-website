In my opinion this piece of code has at least a problem: the entire content is contained in a conditional clause.

Having all the method content inside a conditional clause is a well known anti-pattern, Beck points at the problem in his book Implementation Patterns (Beck. K, Implementation Patterns. Addison-Wesley, p 70-71)
