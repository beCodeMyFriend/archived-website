Last Thursday we celebrated English Day open house at beCode. Every Thursday we switch our language to English in order to practice, learn and become more fluent.

Not many people came, but it was such a success. We spent the whole day speaking English. Even pairing, talking about business or writing emails among us. It's exhausting, but worth it!

We'll keep on celebrating these open house days. So stay tuned to our blog and don't miss next beCode's English Day!


Thanks everybody for coming! =)
